Help
Login or Register

Log in using your username and password.

If you do not have a registration, you will not be able to use the application.

Click on the Register option and enter your data. If you are a doctor, click on I'm a Doctor to enter your data.

After that, log in!
As a User

As a user, you can diagnose any symptoms you are experiencing through the Symptom Checker, write a question to a doctor through the Find a Doctor and check articles written by renowned doctors in Healthy Feed.

Symptom Checker: Here you will select what you are feeling and click on Diagnose. You can quickly search for a symptom through the "Search for a symptom" input. Do not forget to select your gender. This information is necessary for an accurate diagnosis.

Find a Doctor: Here you can write a post that will be seen by a doctor and he can answer your question. Feel free to write as many times as you want!

Healthy Feed: Here will be articles and news posted directly by the medians. If you are someone fascinated by medicine and like good content, this is where you will find it.

 As a Doctor

As a doctor, you can answer questions from users through Reply Questions, write an article or novelty in My Articles and check what other doctors are posting through Healthy Feed.

Reply Questions: Here you can answer questions from users who use our site to receive a diagnosis. They will be happy to have your answer!

My Articles: Write here your articles and news, tell us about your work and add information to our website. Your contribution is very important!

Healthy Feed: Here are the posts posted by doctors registered on the site, including yours. Follow the repercussion of them and interact with other people. Our goal is to foster ideas!
